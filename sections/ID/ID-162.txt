     Maritime torts include injuries to persons,881 damages to prop-
erty arising out of collisions or other negligent acts,882 and violent
dispossession of property.883 The Court has expressed a willingness
to “recogniz[e] products liability, including strict liability, as part of
the general maritime law.” 884 Unlike contract cases, maritime tort
jurisdiction historically depended exclusively upon the commission
of the wrongful act upon navigable waters, regardless of any con-
nection or lack of connection with shipping or commerce.885 The Court
